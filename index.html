About Me

I am a full stack web developer inspired by innovation. Professional and personal experience drive my curiosity and the desire to improve who I am and what I do. From balancing work and school, to freelancing as a handyman and working in construction, I understand that hard work is necessary to transform an idea into a high-quality product.

Moreover, whether by learning the fundamentals of guitar, or improvisation on the saxophone, music has expanded my creative potential and discipline as well. Naturally, coding is where this creativity, determination, and passion meet. These experiences have equipped me with the tools to learn, adapt, and thrive in this constantly evolving industry.
